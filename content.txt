Additional CSS Requirement
You may use any selectors you prefer; however, your stylesheet must clearly demonstrate the following four types of CSS selectors:

At least one HTML tag selector (example: body, h1, p)
At least one descendant selector (example: targeting links inside nav)
At least one class selector
At least one ID selector
You are not restricted to only these selectors, but all four must appear in your CSS.

Failure to demonstrate all four targeting methods will result in a deduction.
